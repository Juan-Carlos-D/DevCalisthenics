Welcome to the Muscle-Up Program!

The DevCalisthenics Muscle-Up Program is designed to help you achieve your first muscle-up by focusing on three key goals: pull strength, dip strength, and transition


Through a series of carefully structured exercises, you will develop essential pulling strength that is essential in achieving your first muscle-up, improve dip strength with exercises such as dips, to help build a solid transition from pull to dip. This program aims to equip calisthenics athletes with the skills and confidence needed to achieve their first muscle-up, setting the stage for long-term fitness and functional strength.
