Welcome to the Beginner Program!

The DevCalisthenics Beginner Program is designed to build a strong foundation in calisthenics by focusing on three key goals: core strength, upper body strength, and body control.


Through a series of carefully structured exercises, you will develop essential core stability that supports all movement, improve upper body strength with exercises such as push-ups and pull-ups, and enhance overall body coordination and balance. This program aims to equip beginners with the skills and confidence needed to progress to more advanced calisthenics movements, setting the stage for long-term fitness and functional strength.
