Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is calisthenics?

Calisthenics is a form of strength training that utilizes an individual's body weight as resistance to perform multi-joint compound movements with little or no equipment.

2. How do I know which program is right for me?

First, decide what goals you want to accomplish. Then, pick a program that closely aligns with that goal. If you are a beginner, it is recommended to start with the beginner program to build a strong foundation.

3. What equipment do I need? Can I workout at home?

You can absolutely do these workouts in the comfort of your home. Some programs may require equipment, but the program description will have the required equipments before any purchases are made.

4. How will I receive my program?

We will email you a PDF that includes everything you need to be successful in your program.

5. What do I do if I lose access to my program?

You can talk to our AI agent, 'Nova', or email us and we'll gladly help you out :)
